IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

NOT INCLUDED IN THIS DATA:
- Detailed flood/tsunami hazard zone maps
- Landslide-prone area designations
- Scenic/environmental protection overlay zones
- Development restriction zones
- Earthquake-resistant zoning requirements (separate building code)
- Setback and parking space requirements
- Latest 2026 Q3-Q4 zoning changes (still being published)

PROPERTY-LEVEL PRECISION:
- Data represents DISTRICT/CBD AVERAGES, not individual properties
- Actual zoning varies by:
  * Specific block location
  * Lot shape and orientation
  * Underground utilities and easements
  * Historical preservation overlay zones
  * Special-use district designations
  * Recently passed ordinance amendments

CRITICAL: Always contact official city planning division (都市計画課)
          before making major property investment or development decisions.
